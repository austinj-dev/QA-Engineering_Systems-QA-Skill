# Compliance & Legal Testing — QA Engineering

## GDPR
- [ ] Privacy policy accessible
- [ ] Cookie consent banner on first visit
- [ ] Cookie preferences saveable
- [ ] Data export (download my data) works
- [ ] Account deletion actually deletes data
- [ ] Right to rectification (edit personal data)

## CCPA
- [ ] Do-not-sell option (if applicable)
- [ ] Data collection disclosure
- [ ] Opt-out mechanism works

## General Legal
- [ ] Terms of service accessible
- [ ] Terms acceptance recorded with timestamp
- [ ] Age verification (if required)
- [ ] Accessibility compliance path documented
- [ ] Third-party data sharing disclosed

## PCI DSS (if payment)
- [ ] No card numbers stored in your database
- [ ] Payment processed via certified provider (Stripe)
- [ ] Payment page uses HTTPS
